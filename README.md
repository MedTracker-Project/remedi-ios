# Medication Tracking App

## Overview
**Medication Tracking App** is a mobile application designed to help users input and monitor their daily medication intake. The app rewards consistent tracking with a **point system**, helping users stay motivated and aware of their habits.

## Description
Users can log what medication they took, how much they took, and when they took it during the day. Each entry contributes to a **reward-based point system**, making medication adherence more engaging and easier to follow.  
The goal is to turn consistent tracking into a positive and beneficial routine.

## Category
**Health & Fitness**

## Mobile Design
This app is designed specifically for **mobile use** to support quick inputs, simple tracking, and easy access to medication history. Mobile UX ensures minimal friction for daily use and real-time updates.

## Story & Purpose
Tracking medication is often tedious and inconsistent. This app enhances that experience by presenting data in an **easier, visual, and motivating way**.  
The built-in point system encourages users to remain consistent while creating a sense of progress and accomplishment.

## Target Market
This app is designed for **anyone taking medication**, whether pills, drops, supplements, or other forms. It offers value for individuals managing daily medications, parents tracking doses for a child, or caregivers supporting others.

## Scope & Features
The app aims to support a core set of intuitive and motivational features, including:

- Daily medication input and tracking  
- Points earned for each medication logged  
- Stats page displaying:
  - Total points earned  
  - Daily streak count  
  - Overall medication history  

Future enhancements may include:
- Push reminders for scheduled doses  
- Custom medication presets  
- Weekly or monthly charts  
- Achievements and rewards
